Plaque frame asset checklist
============================

Frame edge part PNGs are 16x16. Full plaque PNGs may be higher resolution and are mapped across multipart models with UV windows.
Inspect each mount/preset group for readable full-face calligraphy, edge continuity, and hanging hardware.

- town_shop_wood_3w (Town Shop Wood 3W)
  material: weathered oak with rubbed dark stain
  edge: plain raised rail with hand-cut bevels
  corners: small square peg caps
  hanging: iron eyelets and short chain loops
- town_inn_lacquered_4w (Town Inn Lacquered 4W)
  material: red-brown lacquer over smooth wood
  edge: rounded lacquer rail with darker underside
  corners: brass pin clusters
  hanging: dark brass eyelets
- town_notice_board_3w (Town Notice Board 3W)
  material: rough pine board with paper-tack scars
  edge: flat plank frame with uneven nail heads
  corners: black nail plates
  hanging: simple iron straps
- tavern_signboard_4w (Tavern Signboard 4W)
  material: dark oak with warm brass strapping
  edge: thick outer rail and inset inner lip
  corners: diagonal brass corner brackets
  hanging: paired blackened chain rings
- sect_simple_pine_4w (Sect Simple Pine 4W)
  material: pale pine with restrained ink wash
  edge: thin cloud-profile rail
  corners: inked cloud curls
  hanging: wrapped cord loops
- sect_scripture_ornate_4w (Sect Scripture Ornate 4W)
  material: dark lacquer with jade-green inlay
  edge: stepped rail with inner bead
  corners: jade cloud-corner plates
  hanging: green-bronze rings
- lord_manor_heraldry_5w (Lord Manor Heraldry 5W)
  material: polished walnut with enamel heraldic bosses
  edge: wide double rail with mitered corners
  corners: shield-like enamel plates
  hanging: heavy brass lugs
- sect_treasure_gilded_5w_2h (Sect Treasure Gilded 5W 2H)
  material: black lacquer and gilded relief
  edge: monumental stepped frame with raised gilt rim
  corners: gilded cloud-and-pearl bosses
  hanging: three heavy ring mounts for 5w spans
