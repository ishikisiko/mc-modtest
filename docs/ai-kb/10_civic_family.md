# Civic Family Vocabulary

The civic family currently contains `tavern` and `lord_manor` structures.
They are generated by `tools/generate_civic_library.py`, separate from the
default medieval building library and the Chinese courtyard compound loop.

- `great_hall_volume`: tavern main volume with a double-height hall and a partial mezzanine floor.
- `mezzanine`: partial upper floor metadata on a great hall. `covers` names the covered east/west half-plane, and the uncovered half-plane stays open to the roof.
- `tower_volume`: attached lord-manor tower with its own story count, roof, and scoped stairwell.
- `belfry`: tower metadata that requires a hanging `bell` near the tower roof ridge.
- `stable`: tavern shed annex metadata that lays hay flooring, a fence gate, and optional water trough.

Civic interiors resolve role blocks through `INTERIOR_CIVIC`, `FURNITURE`,
`SIGNAGE`, and `HERALDRY` slots in `medieval_village.json`.

## Courtyard family rebuild

The `chinese_courtyard` family is a separate vernacular compound library. Its
built form uses four registry-backed roofs: `chinese_flush_gable` (硬山),
`chinese_overhang_gable` (悬山), `chinese_half_hip` (歇山), and
`chinese_round_ridge` (卷棚). Sub-buildings share the cultivation massing
grammar's `PLATFORM_STONE` 台基 and standoff `COLUMN` 檐廊 slots, populated by
vanilla-only materials in this style.

The shipped 一进 plan has an outer yard and raised main yard separated by one
垂花门. A street-side 影壁 blocks the axial sightline; two 抄手游廊 return from
the gate flanks to the 正房/月台. The six deterministic templates vary
`layout_type`, `main_orientation`, `main_bays`, `roof_grade`, `platform_tier`,
and `gate_type`; water and planting remain minor seeded axes.

## Ground + path layer

The courtyard floor is no longer a single gravel strip floating over air. Two
data-driven passes (`tools/buildgen/compound.py`) rebuild the ground and the
walkable network:

- **Ground layer** (`_place_yard_ground`, courtyard-ground-layer spec): every
  non-building parcel cell gets a solid block at its natural surface y
  (`y = -1` in the outer yard and inner-gate band; `y = plinth_h - 1` on the
  main-yard plinth). Each cell is classified 露天 `open_sky` (default — outer
  yard reads as `grass_block`, main yard reads as paver) or 屋檐下 `under_eave`
  (covered-gallery cells, moon-platform cells, and the 1-cell Chebyshev ring
  around each building footprint — reads as `stone_bricks`). The block comes
  from the style's `GROUND_YARD_OPEN` / `GROUND_YARD_UNDER_EAVE` slots; both
  resolve to vanilla `minecraft:` ids under `--profile vanilla`.
- **Path network** (`_route_complete_path`, courtyard-path-network spec): a
  multi-source BFS seeds from every door-front, every water feature / fish jar
  (snapped to one walkable approach cell per node), one entry per planting bed,
  the moon-platform apron, and the street-gate entry cell. The reached cells
  form one connected walkable surface written as `GROUND_PATH` at the same y as
  the ground tile, with `PROTECTED` so material variation never re-colors it.
  Path cells never overwrite a door-front (the building's own step owns that
  cell).
- **Plinth transition** (`_place_plinth_stairs`): where a path cell at
  `y = plinth_h - 1` is 4-adjacent to a path cell at `y = -1`, a single
  `stone_brick_stairs[facing=<toward plinth>, half=bottom]` replaces the outer
  path cell so the player steps up without jumping. Small-courtyard units (no
  plinth) skip this pass.

`validate_compound` / `validate_small_courtyard` enforce the five new error
codes — `endpoint_unreachable`, `ground_layer_hole`, `ground_kind_mismatch`,
`plinth_edge_missing_stair`, `path_overlaps_building_door` — and the library
report carries `ground_cells`, `endpoint_count`, and `stair_cells` stats.

## 江南大宅 (chinese_mansion) compound family

The `chinese_mansion` family ships a 3-进 江南大宅 layout as six NBTs
(`chinese_mansion_001..006.nbt`). It is the first compound family realized with
the enclosure-planning skeleton: buildings are placed as orientation-bearing
masses first, each role gets a form-rule facing, and the 进 are derived as the
negative space those buildings enclose. Generated by
`tools/generate_compound_library.py --group chinese_mansion --count 6 --base-seed 20260618`.

**Plan sequence (south gate, inward enclosure axis):**

```
街 → 门屋 gate_house (through-building, not a wall hole)
           → 照壁 (off-axis 照壁侧立) → 前院
           → 仪门 (≥3-cell passage) → 主院 (敞厅 open_hall + 厢房)
           → 二门 → 后院 (绣楼/藏书楼 tower_house × 1 or 2)
           → 花园 (假山 + 水池 + 亭)
```

**Enclosure skeleton:** `gate_house` and optional 倒座 anchor the south edge and
face north into the 前院; `open_hall` anchors the main yard north edge and faces
south; west/east `side_wing` slots face inward; `tower_house` faces the 后院 it
serves. The orientation mechanism relocates the door wall only — it does not
rotate or mirror the footprint/roof axis. Every building door-front is a path
endpoint, so the gravel backbone is routed from the gate-house inner opening to
each door before voxel-walkability validates the result.

**Variant axes:** `courtyard_size ∈ {small, medium, large}`, `gate_form ∈
{recessed, flush, paifang}`, `garden_scale ∈ {small, large}`, `tower_count ∈
{1, 2}`, `roof_grade`, `main_bays ∈ {3, 5}`. Six deterministic templates cover
all combinations; 6 seeds hit all 6 templates.

**Sub-building archetypes:**
- `main_hall`: 正房 on raised 台基 plinth; same pipeline as 一进 main_hall
- `open_hall`: 敞厅; front facade resolves through `FACADE_OPEN` (columns, no
  full front wall); placed on the main-yard plinth and records south facing
- `side_wing`: 厢房; uses the same north-south footprint/ridge as 一进, but the
  door wall is relocated east/west by role so it faces the yard
- `front_row`: 倒座 beside the gate-house when the selected footprint has room;
  door wall relocates north so it faces 前院 rather than the street
- `gate_house`: 门屋 through-building on the south perimeter; `gate_type` selects
  its footprint, and the perimeter wall gap exactly matches its side walls
- `tower_house`: 绣楼 / 藏书楼; `stories=2` via multi-story-massing; off-axis
  in 后院 with the door facing its yard
- `garden_pavilion`: 亭; `chinese_round_ridge` roof; 4-column standoff; placed
  on or near 假山 peak

**Garden elements:** `garden_rockery` (假山: `myvillage:rockery_block` with
variant/moss; 5 roles: peak/slope/base/corner/standalone), `garden_pond` (水池:
freeform noise shoreline, `minecraft:water` at y=-1), `garden_pavilion` 亭,
汀步 stepping stones across the pond.

**Style profile:** `tools/buildgen/styles/chinese_mansion.json` — adds
`FACADE_OPEN`, `GARDEN_PATH`, `ROCKERY_STONE`, `GARDEN_PAVEMENT`, `POND_STONE`
slots beyond the base courtyard vocabulary. Primary `GROUND_PATH` is `gravel`
(not `stone_bricks`, which is reserved for `GROUND_YARD_UNDER_EAVE`).

**Validation:** `validate_mansion` (not `validate_compound`). Checks: gate-house
presence and south-perimeter straddling, form-rule facings per slot, every
door-front on or adjacent to the path, 进-sequence adjacency (仪门 borders 前院+
主院; 二门 borders 主院+后院), perimeter, garden parcels, ground holes, silhouette
spread, and voxel walkability from the gate-house inner opening through all
yards to 花园 endpoints. Report at
`reports/chinese_mansion_compound_library_report.json` with `facing_per_slot`
and `door_reachable_rate`.

## Compound planning skeleton

`chinese_mansion` now uses the enclosure + orientation skeleton. The
`chinese_courtyard` and embedded `small_courtyard` planners still use the older
band-coupled planner for this change; propagating the skeleton to those families
is tracked in `docs/ai-kb/14_deferred_roadmap.md`.

**Voxel-walkability validator:** The compound pipeline now includes 3D BFS
(`_voxel_walk_bfs`) from the gate-entry standable column. Error codes:
`voxel_unreachable_door:<archetype>`, `voxel_unreachable_endpoint:<x,z>`,
`voxel_step_cliff:<a>-><b>`, `voxel_blocked_by_solid:<x,z>`. These replace the
old 2D graph reachability check for both `chinese_courtyard` and
`chinese_mansion`.

**Mod decor blocks:** The `myvillage:rockery_block` is a self-namespace block
shipped with the mod (not an external mod dependency). It is exempt from
`mod_block_fallbacks.json` under the `myvillage:` self-namespace rule. Under
`--profile vanilla` it resolves to `minecraft:stone` via the style slot fallback
chain. See [mod-decor-block-family](../../openspec/changes/rebuild-jiangnan-mansion/specs/mod-decor-block-family/)
for the registration scaffolding.
