# Cultivation Primer

The playable order is: complete [Initiation](./getting_started/initiation.md) by awakening your spiritual root at the testing stele, inherit Basic Breathing, then follow the [Cultivation Loop](./getting_started/cultivation_loop.md) to gain progress first, consolidate stability second, and finally complete deterministic one-stage advancements.

<SubPages icons={true} />

## Current Release Boundary

- Awakening and inheritance are separate steps. Learning the technique does not automatically grant progress or stability.
- Normal and spirit-stone meditation are settled by the server. Progress grows until its cap; stability begins with a later batch after progress is full.
- Both progress and stability must meet their current-stage caps before deterministic advancement can begin.
- **Qi Refining IV is the current playable release ceiling.** Progression at Qi Refining V or later, Foundation Establishment gameplay, pills, cultivation facilities, tribulation, death from old age, and reincarnation are not implemented.

Read [Initiation](./getting_started/initiation.md) first, then continue to the [Cultivation Loop](./getting_started/cultivation_loop.md).
