# Garden Rockery

## Purpose

This spec defines 假山 (rockery) as the first instance of the `mod-decor-block-family` protocol, plus the `garden_pond` (freeform water) and `garden_pavilion` (亭) parcels that complete the 江南园林 water-mountain vocabulary. The 假山 is realized as a depth-2 mod-owned block (`myvillage:rockery_block`) with pre-baked sub-block-precision models and VoxelShapes; the 水池 is realized as freeform water using a deterministic value-noise binalization; the 亭 reuses the cultivation pavilion geometry.

## Requirements

### Requirement: `myvillage:rockery_block` carries a role-based variant catalog

The `myvillage:rockery_block` class SHALL ship a variant catalog of at least 30 and at most 50 variants, grouped by role:
- `peak` (峰顶) — 8-10 variants. Tall, narrow, with overhanging top. VoxelShape: solid-blocking up to peak height (NOT standable on top).
- `slope` (山腰) — 8-10 variants. Mid-height, sloped surface. VoxelShape: solid with a standable flat top face (player can stand mid-mountain).
- `base` (山脚) — 6-8 variants. Low, broad, base-of-mountain. VoxelShape: solid with a standable top face.
- `corner` (转角) — 4-6 variants. L-shaped or curved, for rockery corners and bends. VoxelShape: solid with a standable top face.
- `standalone` (孤赏石) — 4-6 variants. Single specimen stones (太湖石-class), irregular shape, used as 汀步 (stepping stones) or isolated ornamental stones. VoxelShape: solid-blocking, narrow.

Each variant SHALL be unique; no two variants in the same role SHALL share the same voxel field.

#### Scenario: The variant catalog covers all five roles

- **WHEN** the variant catalog is enumerated
- **THEN** at least one variant SHALL exist for each of `peak`, `slope`, `base`, `corner`, `standalone`
- **AND** the total variant count SHALL be between 30 and 50.

#### Scenario: A peak variant is not standable on top

- **WHEN** a `myvillage:rockery_block[variant=peak_01]` is placed
- **THEN** the VoxelShape SHALL be solid-blocking up to the peak height
- **AND** no flat standable top face SHALL exist in the VoxelShape above the base.

#### Scenario: A slope variant is standable on top

- **WHEN** a `myvillage:rockery_block[variant=slope_01]` is placed
- **THEN** the VoxelShape SHALL include a flat standable top face at the slope's mid-height
- **AND** the player SHALL be able to stand on that top face.

### Requirement: Variant models are generated offline from a 16×16×16 voxel field

Each variant's model JSON SHALL be generated by an offline Python tool (`tools/buildgen/rockery_models.py`) that:
1. Derives a deterministic 16×16×16 voxel field for the variant using a heightfield + value-noise paradigm (mirroring `sect_mountain.py`'s `DerivedMountain.height(x, z)`), parameterized by the variant's role and a per-variant seed.
2. Marks each sub-voxel as solid or air based on the heightfield threshold plus per-voxel value noise (for surface texture / 孔洞 / 皱褶).
3. Converts the solid voxels to a model JSON (one or more cube elements per merged solid region, with stone / andesite / moss-overlay textures).
4. Computes the merged `VoxelShape` (AABB union with greedy merge to keep AABB count ≤ 32).
5. Writes the model JSON to `assets/myvillage/models/block/rockery_block/<variant>.json` and the VoxelShape entry to the Java lookup table.

#### Scenario: A variant's model and VoxelShape agree on solid cells

- **WHEN** the offline tool generates a `peak_03` variant
- **THEN** the model JSON's solid cube elements SHALL match the VoxelShape's AABBs at sub-block granularity
- **AND** no cell marked solid in the voxel field SHALL be air in the model OR missing from the VoxelShape.

#### Scenario: The model JSON's AABB count is within vanilla's soft limit

- **WHEN** the offline tool generates any variant's model JSON
- **THEN** the merged AABB count SHALL be ≤ 32
- **AND** the variant's collision check SHALL remain performant.

### Requirement: 假山 placement is driven by a heightfield over the parcel bbox

A `garden_rockery` parcel SHALL be placed by `tools/buildgen/rockery.py` (`derive_rockery(seed, bbox, params)`), which computes a heightfield over the parcel bbox and assigns each cell a variant role based on the cell's height relative to the parcel:
- Cells in the top height-decile → `peak` role.
- Cells in the middle height-deciles → `slope` role.
- Cells in the bottom height-decile (where the rockery meets the ground) → `base` role.
- Cells at the bbox perimeter corners → `corner` role.
- Cells designated as 汀步 (stepping stones) → `standalone` role.

The heightfield SHALL be deterministic given the seed and bbox (reusing `sect_mountain.py`'s `_hash2` / `_noise` helpers). The variant selection within a role SHALL be deterministic given the seed and cell coordinates. The `moss_level` SHALL be assigned based on cell height: low cells → `none`, mid cells → `light`, peak cells → `heavy`.

#### Scenario: A 假山 parcel places variants by height role

- **WHEN** a `garden_rockery` parcel is generated with a seed and bbox
- **THEN** every cell in the bbox SHALL be assigned a variant role
- **AND** high cells SHALL receive `peak` variants
- **AND** low cells SHALL receive `base` variants.

#### Scenario: The same seed and bbox produces the same 假山

- **WHEN** `derive_rockery` is called twice with the same `(seed, bbox, params)`
- **THEN** the two resulting 假山 placements SHALL be identical (same variant per cell, same moss_level).

### Requirement: 假山 composes with 水池 (山脚入水)

When a `garden_rockery` parcel and a `garden_pond` parcel share a bbox boundary, the rockery's `base` variants SHALL be placed at the boundary cells, partially overhanging the pond's water. The overhanging base variants SHALL NOT displace the water at the overhanging cells — the water is written first, then the rockery base is placed on top at y=0+ (the rockery base rests in the water, reading as 山脚入水).

#### Scenario: 山脚入水 at the pond boundary

- **WHEN** a `garden_rockery` parcel meets a `garden_pond` parcel
- **THEN** the rockery's `base`-role cells at the boundary SHALL be placed at the pond edge
- **AND** the water at the boundary cells SHALL remain at y=-1 (below the rockery base).

### Requirement: 水池 is freeform via deterministic value-noise binalization

A `garden_pond` parcel SHALL be placed as a freeform water body, NOT a strict rectangle. The shoreline SHALL be derived by a 2D value-noise binalization over the pond bbox: for each cell in the bbox, evaluate a deterministic noise function (seed + cell coordinates); cells with noise above a threshold are water, cells below are shore. The binalization SHALL be deterministic given the seed and bbox. The water SHALL be written at y=-1 with `minecraft:water`, surrounded by shore cells at y=0 (vanilla ground). Isolated 1-2 cell water or land pockets SHALL be filled (water pocket → land, land pocket → water) to avoid unreachable islands or odd puddles.

#### Scenario: The pond shoreline is irregular

- **WHEN** a `garden_pond` parcel is generated with a seed and bbox
- **THEN** the shoreline SHALL NOT be a rectangle
- **AND** the shoreline SHALL vary across cells (no two adjacent shore cells at the same offset from the bbox center).

#### Scenario: The same seed produces the same shoreline

- **WHEN** the pond binalization is run twice with the same `(seed, bbox)`
- **THEN** the two shorelines SHALL be identical.

#### Scenario: Isolated pockets are filled

- **WHEN** the binalization produces an isolated 1-2 cell water pocket surrounded by land
- **THEN** the pocket SHALL be filled with land
- **AND** when it produces an isolated 1-2 cell land pocket surrounded by water, the pocket SHALL be filled with water.

### Requirement: 亭 (garden pavilion) reuses cultivation pavilion geometry with a 卷棚 roof

A `garden_pavilion` parcel SHALL be a small open-sided 亭 placed in the 花园, either standalone on the ground or on a rockery peak. The 亭 SHALL reuse the cultivation pavilion geometry pattern (4 standoff columns resolving through the `COLUMN` slot, a square plan, an open-sided eave), with a `chinese_round_ridge` (卷棚) roof. The 亭 SHALL NOT have walls.

#### Scenario: A standalone 亭 in the 花园

- **WHEN** a `garden_pavilion` parcel is placed on the 花园 ground
- **THEN** it SHALL have 4 standoff columns at the `COLUMN` slot
- **AND** it SHALL have a `chinese_round_ridge` roof
- **AND** no walls SHALL be placed.

#### Scenario: A 亭 on a rockery peak

- **WHEN** a `garden_pavilion` parcel is placed on a `garden_rockery` peak
- **THEN** the 亭 SHALL rest on the rockery's standable top
- **AND** the rockery's VoxelShape at the 亭 footprint SHALL be standable.

### Requirement: 花园 features are voxel-walkable from the 后院 exit

Every `garden_pond` adjacent shore cell, every `garden_rockery` adjacent cell, every `garden_pavilion` interior cell, and every `tower_house` second-story stairwell SHALL be reachable from the 后院 exit via a `GARDEN_PATH` 曲径 that passes `courtyard-voxel-walkability`. 汀步 (stepping stones across the pond) SHALL be `myvillage:rockery_block[variant=standalone]` cells placed at standable y, voxel-walkable as a path across the water.

#### Scenario: The 花园 is end-to-end walkable

- **WHEN** `validate_compound` runs on a `chinese_mansion` compound with a 花园
- **THEN** every `garden_pond` shore, every `garden_rockery` adjacent cell, and every `garden_pavilion` interior SHALL be in the voxel-walkable visited set
- **AND** 汀步 cells SHALL be standable.

### Requirement (FUTURE EXTENSION): 蹬道 (climbing-path) variant for 可登假山

A future extension SHALL add a `蹬道` (climbing-path) variant that places `stone_brick_stairs` ascending the rockery surface from `base` to `peak`, making the peak standable. This requirement is captured for design continuity; it is NOT validated by any current validator and SHALL NOT be implemented in the `rebuild-jiangnan-mansion` change.

#### Scenario (FUTURE): A 蹬道 variant makes the peak standable

- **FUTURE: WHEN** a future change adds the 蹬道 variant and a rockery is generated with the climbing-path option
- **FUTURE: THEN** `stone_brick_stairs` SHALL ascend the rockery surface from `base` to `peak`
- **FUTURE: AND** the peak's top SHALL become standable.

### Requirement: A hero 假山 is ingested from a micro-voxel JSON

The offline tool (`tools/buildgen/rockery_models.py`) SHALL parse the named hero
rockery source at `docs/rockery_compressed.json`: a 48×48×48 micro-cube field
(3×3×3 full blocks, 1 micro-cube = 1/16 block) encoded as RLE rows per y-layer,
with palette values `a`=air, `s`=stone, `m`=mossy stone, `w`=water, `g`=grass,
`t`=oak log, and `l`=oak leaves.

#### Scenario: The micro-grid decodes and slices losslessly

- **WHEN** the hero JSON is parsed
- **THEN** every layer row SHALL expand to exactly 48 cells
- **AND** every non-air micro-cube SHALL map to exactly one full-block cell using
  `(x//16, y//16, z//16)` and one cell-local coordinate using
  `(x%16, y%16, z%16)`
- **AND** fully-air cells SHALL be dropped.

### Requirement: Each hero rock cell bakes one isolated variant

For every full-block cell containing `s` or `m`, the tool SHALL bake one
`hero_taihu_*` `rockery_block` variant. Hero variants SHALL be registered for
models, blockstates, and collision lookup but SHALL be excluded from the generic
role-sampling catalog, so the generic `peak`/`slope`/`base`/`corner`/
`standalone` counts remain unchanged.

#### Scenario: Stone and moss preserve the source material masks

- **WHEN** a hero cell contains stone and mossy-stone micro-cubes
- **THEN** the two masks SHALL be greedy-merged separately and use the shared
  `swatch_stone` and `swatch_mossy` textures respectively
- **AND** the combined mask SHALL drive the matching `VoxelShape`.

### Requirement: The hero 假山 is a stacked 3×3×3 cluster

For a parcel tagged `hero=taihu`, `place_garden_rockery` SHALL stamp the baked
cells at their full-block `(dx,dy,dz)` coordinates instead of invoking the
generic 2D heightfield path.

#### Scenario: The mass stacks and supports the summit 亭

- **WHEN** the hero cluster is placed
- **THEN** `myvillage:rockery_block` SHALL occupy at least three distinct Y layers
- **AND** the top rock cell SHALL expose a flat standable face
- **AND** the garden pavilion SHALL be placeable on that summit
- **AND** mansion voxel-walkability validation SHALL report no `voxel_*` errors.

### Requirement: Hero vegetation and sculpted water preserve micro scale

Grass, trunk and leaf materials SHALL be baked as visual-only material masks in
the summit hero model, remain inside its 16³ cell, and SHALL NOT contribute to
the rock-derived collision shape. The tree SHALL read as a miniature leaning
bonsai with visible branches and asymmetric layered foliage pads.

The sealed foot pool SHALL use contained `minecraft:water` source blocks. The
grotto, stepped cascade and embedded rock-foot pool SHALL use the source `w`
micro-voxel mask as tinted, translucent, non-fluid model geometry. No
flowing-water (`level>0`) block SHALL be baked into the placement.

#### Scenario: The standalone specimen is self-contained

- **WHEN** `/myvillage place hero_rockery` is used
- **THEN** the structure SHALL contain its own sealed source-water basin
- **AND** its hero cells SHALL all have `waterlogged=false`
- **AND** the placement SHALL contain no full-block summit grass/tree dressing
- **AND** the placement SHALL contain no fixed exterior `rockery_cascade` column
- **AND** the source water masks SHALL form one continuous route from the
  mountain-interior grotto to the embedded foot pool.

### Requirement: Hero realization is deterministic and byte-stable

The fixed hero JSON SHALL introduce no per-seed noise. Regenerating the specimen
SHALL produce identical model assets and an identical placement record, guarded
by a committed SHA fixture.

#### Scenario: Regeneration matches the fixture

- **WHEN** the hero generator runs twice against the same source JSON
- **THEN** both placement hashes and hero-asset rollup hashes SHALL be identical
- **AND** they SHALL match the committed hero-rockery baseline fixture.
