## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Chinese courtyard sub-building archetypes
The generator SHALL support the Chinese sub-building archetypes `main_hall` (正房), `side_wing` (厢房), and `front_row` (倒座房), classified as housing/civic archetype families that serve town generation by composing courtyard compounds rather than standalone village templates. These archetypes SHALL use the Chinese style profile and SHALL NOT reuse Western archetype blueprints such as `small_house`. The `main_hall` MAY be generated with two stories using the multi-story massing capability; `side_wing` and `front_row` SHALL be single story.

#### Scenario: A main hall is generated as a compound slot
- **WHEN** a compound building slot requests a `main_hall`
- **THEN** the archetype SHALL build a `MassingGraph` using the Chinese style profile
- **AND** it MAY have two stories via the multi-story capability
- **AND** it SHALL NOT reuse the `small_house` massing.

#### Scenario: Wings and front row are single story Chinese buildings
- **WHEN** `side_wing` or `front_row` is generated
- **THEN** the archetype SHALL build a single-story `MassingGraph` using the Chinese style profile.

### Requirement: A compound composes multiple sub-building graphs
A courtyard compound SHALL be composed of multiple sub-building `MassingGraph`s arranged by the compound layer. Each sub-building SHALL be generated by the existing per-building pass pipeline before the compound layer places it into the parcel.

#### Scenario: Sub-buildings are generated then placed
- **WHEN** a compound is generated
- **THEN** each building slot SHALL run the per-building passes to produce a sub-building
- **AND** the compound layer SHALL place each generated sub-building at its slot position.
